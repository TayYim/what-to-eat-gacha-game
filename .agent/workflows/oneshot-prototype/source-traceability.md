# Source and Research Traceability

## Source Requirement

For all claims based on internet research, include source links or citations.
Use current, primary, or authoritative sources where possible.

## Claim Categories

Clearly distinguish between:

- Verified facts
- Inferences
- Assumptions
- Product opinions
- Implementation decisions

Do not present assumptions as facts.

## Decision Traceability

When a researched fact influences scope, architecture, dependency selection, or
build-vs-buy decisions, record the source and the decision it supports.

## Unresolved Traceability

If a claim cannot be verified, label it as an assumption or omit it. A prototype
cannot satisfy the final review gate while it has unresolved source-traceability
issues for claims based on research.
